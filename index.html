WIMSATX 2024

Empowering Excellence: Women in Mathematics Workshop in San Antonio

September 13-14, 2024

University of Texas at San Antonio

Empowering Excellence: Women in Mathematics Workshop in San Antonio (WIMSATX 2024) is a two-day event taking place on September 13-14, 2024, at the University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA). This workshop aims to bring together undergraduate and graduate female students to explore research opportunities and various career paths in mathematics. The workshop will feature plenary speakers from academia and research laboratories, who will discuss cutting-edge research and share insights into different career pathways. Additionally, the event will provide a supportive environment for students to showcase their research through poster presentations and lightning talks, facilitating interactions between students and established mathematicians who can serve as mentors and role models.

Inclusivity

The Empowering Excellence: Women in Mathematics Workshop in San Antonio is committed to promoting equality, diversity, and inclusion for all participants. Any violations of the workshop's non-discrimination policy should be reported to the organizers. By upholding these principles, the workshop can foster an empowering experience for everyone. Should you encounter any situation during the conference that makes you feel unwelcome, we encourage you to reach out to one of the organizers, who will assist you to the best of our ability.
