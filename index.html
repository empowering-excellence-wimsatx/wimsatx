Empowering Excellence: Women in Mathematics Workshop in San Antonio


September 13-15, 2024
University of Texas at San Antonio

**Application for the workshop is OPEN and FREE, with funding support!  Please apply here.**

Our workshop offers an exciting opportunity for undergraduate and graduate female students to explore various career paths and opportunities in mathematics, where you can
  • hear from esteemed speakers in academia and research labs about different career pathways in mathematics
  • receive tips on graduate school applications and summer research opportunities
  • showcase your own projects through poster presentations in a supportive environment and win a poster award! (Why present a poster at a conference?)
  • learn how to make the most of academic conferences and experience a professional conference firsthand with our funding support
  • build connections and expand your professional network with established mathematicians and like-minded peers

Accessibility

We are committed to equal access and opportunity for all participants. For ADA Disability Resources at UTSA please visit the ADA website.

Inclusivity

We are committed to promoting equality, diversity, and inclusion for all participants. Any violations of the workshop's non-discrimination policy should be reported to the organizers. By upholding these principles, the workshop can foster an empowering experience for everyone. Should you encounter any situation during the conference that makes you feel unwelcome, we encourage you to reach out to one of the organizers, who will assist you to the best of our ability.
